# Quant Finance Projects

## Market Data Ingestion and Lakehouse
- Context: Diverse vendor feeds with varying SLAs.
- Approach: Stream + batch ingestion, schema registry, Delta/Iceberg tables, late data handling.
- Outcome: Reliable, queryable history; cheaper backfills and faster research.

## Signal Research Toolkit
- Context: Fragmented research workflows.
- Approach: Standardized factor library, cross-sectional testing, risk model integration.
- Outcome: Reproducible studies; faster iteration.
